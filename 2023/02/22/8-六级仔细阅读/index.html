选词填空

  • a sutdy suggest 一个研究表明

  • It is noteworthy that the findings held even after the researchers considerd factors that could affect the link,including whether participants had health conditions such as heart disease or cancer, or whether they experienced depression.
    值得注意的是，即使在研究人员考虑了可能会影响二者之间关联的因素之后，研究结论依然成立，这些因素包括研究对象是否有心脏病、癌症等健康问题，或者他们是否曾有过抑郁症

  • The results add to a growing body of evidence that …… 该研究的结果进一步证实了……

  • premature death 过早死亡

  • the researchers conceded…… 研究者承认……

  • psychological trait 心里素质

  • henceforth 从今以后

  • correlation 关联

  • spiral 螺旋线

  • plausibly 似乎是真的
